About Me

I grew up on a dairy farm in Northwest Arkansas and moved to central Texas in 2018. Like a lot of people, I lost my job when the pandemic started in 2020. This was the point that I found my passion for programming, as I struggled to find a new career and learn new skills. I'd love to help other people find their passion, and I can do so by building a showcase that you can put your dreams on display with. Whether you need a website built, or a software engineer to join your team, let me show you how I can help!
